We'd like to thank some people and projects who've helped us along the way:

- [All our contributors](https://github.com/avitorio/outstatic/graphs/contributors) with a special thanks to: [jakobo](https://github.com/jakobo), [ZiyadBoshima](https://github.com/ZiyadBoshima) and [praveenjuge](https://github.com/praveenjuge). You all rock!
- [@steventey](https://x.com/steventey) for the support and for creating and allowing us to use part of [Novel](https://novel.sh) as our editor.

Outstatic is built on the following stack:

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) – framework
- [GitHub](https://github.com/) – content storage and auth
- [Tiptap](https://tiptap.dev/) – text editor
- [OpenAI](https://openai.com/) - AI completions
- [Vercel AI SDK](https://sdk.vercel.ai/docs) – AI library
- [TailwindCSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) – styles
- [Novel](https://novel.sh/) – AI completions and slash command
